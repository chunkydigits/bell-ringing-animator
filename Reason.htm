The Bell Ringing Animator       

 

WHY ?

Why would I spend my third year at university fiddling with a bunch of bells? Well at first it started out as the fact that I was given my fourth choice project that I had selected, this meant I didn't have a choice on what I spent my third year doing.

I actually really enjoyed the project towards the end of the programming, I learned a lot of things about Java, of which I would know very little about now, if I hadn't been made to do a load of bells.

So look through the site and think of how hard I've had to work to get it working.

HOW TO USE THE BELL RINGING ANIMATOR
PLAYING A TUNE

When playing a tune on the Bell Ringing Animator, you have many different options on how to play. You can use the number pad keys from 1 - 8, you can use the keypad numbers from 1 - 8 or you can click on the bells and play that way.

_____________________________________________________________

RECORDING

-  Press "RECORD" when you are ready - the record button should turn red.

-  then play the tune that you wish to record - the gaps will record themselves.

-  when you have finished playing the tune, press "STOP"

_____________________________________________________________

PLAYING BACK A RECORDED TUNE

-  When you wish to play back what you have recorded, press the "PLAY RECORDED" button

- You have just recorded and played a tune

_____________________________________________________________

CONVERTING

When converting a tune, use the radio buttons below the conversion panel to specify when the note is a sharp or whether the note is a crotchet or a quaver.

The press in the note that you wish to convert.

When you have finished entering your tune, press the "CONVERT" button and the tune will be ready to play back.

_____________________________________________________________ 

PLAYING BACK A CONVERTED TUNE

When playing back a tune, firstly make sure you have entered your tune correctly by scrolling along the bell scrolling panel in the centre. Make sure you have pressed the "CONVERT" button and then press the "PLAY" button. This should invoke the playback mechanism.

_____________________________________________________________

SCROLLING ALONG THE "IMAGES"

When viewing your notes on the image pane, you can start from one end and scroll left or  scroll to the full left first and see you notes from the beginning. The two buttons with |< and >| displayed on them mean to scroll to full left or full right, respectively.

The buttons that bare the < and > mean to scroll one note along to the left or the right respectively.
